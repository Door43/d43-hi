====== यरुशलेम ======

===== कुछ तथ्य : =====

‘यरुशलेम’ इस्राएल देश का एक नगर था। यह आज भी आस्तित्व में है।

  *

राजा दाऊद ने यरुशलेम को कनानवासियों से जीत कर उसे अपनी राजधानी बनाया।

  *

The temple was built in Jerusalem by David's son, King Solomon.

  *

God sent the Babylonians to destroy Jerusalem because of Israel's sin. However, he allowed them to return and rebuild Jerusalem 70 years later.

  *

Jerusalem was the city where Jesus the Messiah was put on trial and sentenced to die on a cross.

  *

Jerusalem was one of the main places where Jesus spent time with his disciples after he came back to life, and before returning to heaven.

[See also: **[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/key-terms/david|David]]**, **[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/key-terms/solomon|Solomon]]**, **[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/key-terms/messiah|Messiah]]**, **[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/key-terms/jesus|Jesus]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/17-05|[17-05]]]** David conquered **Jerusalem**  and made it his capital city.
  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/18-02|[18-02]]]** In **Jerusalem**, Solomon built the Temple for which his father David had planned and gathered materials.
  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/20-07|[20-07]]]** They (Babylonians) captured the city of **Jerusalem**, destroyed the Temple, and took away all the treasures of the city and the Temple.
  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/20-12|[20-12]]]** So, after seventy years in exile, a small group of Jews returned to the city of **Jerusalem**  in Judah.
  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/38-01|[38-01]]]** About three years after Jesus first began preaching and teaching publicly, Jesus told his disciples that he wanted to celebrate this Passover with them in **Jerusalem**, and that he would be killed there.
  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/38-02|[38-02]]]** After Jesus and the disciples arrived in **Jerusalem**, Judas went to the Jewish leaders and offered to betray Jesus to them in exchange for money.
  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/42-08|[42-08]]]** “It was also written in the scriptures that my disciples will proclaim that everyone should repent in order to receive forgiveness for their sins. They will do this starting in **Jerusalem**, and then go to all people groups everywhere. ”
  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/42-11|[42-11]]]** Forty days after Jesus rose from the dead, he told his disciples, “Stay in **Jerusalem**  until you receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you.”
