====== एलिय्याह ======

===== कुछ तथ्य : =====

यहोवा के सबसे प्रमुख नबियों में से एक एलिय्याह नबी था। उसने नबूवत उस समय की जब इस्राएल के उत्तरी राज्य में अहाब राज्य करता था।

  *

Elijah confronted Ahab because Ahab encouraged the people to worship false gods.

  *

God provided food and shelter for Elijah in miraculous ways.

  *

God did many miracles through Elijah and showed Israel that Yahweh is the only true God.

  *

Elijah confronted the 450 prophets of Baal and demonstrated that Baal is a false god. As a result, the people in the kingdom of Israel killed the prophets of Baal and temporarily worshiped only Yahweh.

  *

Elijah did not die–he was taken up to heaven alive. Elijah and Moses then appeared and talked with Jesus hundreds of years later when Jesus was getting ready to die in Jerusalem.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/19-02|[19-02]]]****Elijah**  was a prophet when Ahab was king over the kingdom of Israel.
  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/19-02|[19-02]]]****Elijah**  said to Ahab, “There will be no rain or dew in the kingdom of Israel until I say so.”
  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/19-03|[19-03]]]** God told **Elijah**  to go to a stream in the wilderness to hide from Ahab who wanted to kill him. Every morning and every evening, birds would bring him bread and meat.
  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/19-04|[19-04]]]** But they took care of **Elijah**, and God provided for them so that their flour jar and their bottle of oil never became empty.
  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/19-05|[19-05]]]** After three and a half years, God told **Elijah**  to return to the kingdom of Israel and speak with Ahab because he was going to send rain again.
  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/19-07|[19-07]]]** Then **Elijah**  said to the prophets of Baal, “Kill a bull and prepare it as a sacrifice, but do not light the fire.”
  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/19-12|[19-12]]]** Then **Elijah**  said, “Do not let any of the prophets of Baal escape!”
  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/36-03|[36-03]]]** Then Moses and the prophet **Elijah**  appeared. These men had lived hundreds of years before this. They talked with Jesus about his death that would soon happen in Jerusalem.
