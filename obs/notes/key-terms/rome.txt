====== रोम ======

==== (रोम, रोमी) ====

===== कुछ तथ्य: =====

आधुनिक समय में रोम इटली का राजधानी शहर है।

  *

यीशु व प्रेरितों के दिनो में रोम पूरे रोमी साम्राज्य का केंद्र हुआ करता था।

  *

The Roman Empire ruled over all of the regions around the Mediterranean Sea, including Israel.

  *

The term, “Roman” refers to anything relating to the regions that the government in Rome controlled, including Roman citizens and Roman officials.

  *

The Apostle Paul was taken to the city of Rome as a prisoner because he preached the good news about Jesus.

[See also: **[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/key-terms/pilate|Pilate]]**, **[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/key-terms/paul|Paul]]**, **[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/key-terms/good-news|good news]]**]

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/23-04|[23-04]]]** When the time was near for Mary to give birth, the **Roman**  government told everyone to go for a census to the town where their ancestors had lived.
  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/32-06|[32-06]]]** Then Jesus asked the demon, “What is your name?” He replied, “My name is Legion, because we are many.” (A “legion” was a group of several thousand soldiers in the **Roman**  army.)
  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/39-09|[39-09]]]** Early the next morning, the Jewish leaders brought Jesus to the **Roman**  governor, Pilate, hoping to have Jesus killed.
  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/39-12|[39-12]]]** The **Roman**  soldiers whipped Jesus and put a royal robe and a crown made of thorns on him. Then they mocked him by saying, “Look, the King of the Jews!”
