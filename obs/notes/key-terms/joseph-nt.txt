====== यूसुफ (न.नि.) ======

===== कुछ तथ्य : =====

यीशु के मानव पिता का नाम यूसुफ था, और उसने यीशु को बेटे के समान पाला था।

  * यूसुफ, राजा दाऊद का वंशज था।
  * यूसुफ की मरियम नाम की लड़की से सगाई हुई थी, जिसे परमेश्वर ने यीशु मसीह की माता होने के लिए चुना था।
  * यूसुफ को एक स्वर्गदूत ने स्वप्न में बताया था कि पवित्र आत्मा के आश्चर्यकर्म के द्वारा मरियम गर्भवती हुई है, और मरियम का बेटा परमेश्वर का बेटा था।
  * यूसुफ ने स्वर्गदूत की बात पर विश्वास किया और परमेश्वर की आज्ञा का पालन करते हुए मरियम को अपनी पत्नी बनाया।
  * यीशु का जन्म होने तक यूसुफ मरियम के साथ नहीं सोया।

===== बाइबल की कहानियों में से उदाहरण : =====

  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/22-04|[22-04]]]** She (Mary) was a virgin and was engaged to be married to a man named **Joseph**.

  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/23-01|[23-01]]]****Joseph**, the man Mary was engaged to, was a righteous man. When he heard that Mary was pregnant, he knew it was not his baby. He did not want to shame her, so he planned to quietly divorce her.

  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/23-02|[23-02]]]** The angel said, “**Joseph**, do not be afraid to take Mary as your wife. The baby in her body is from the Holy Spirit. She will give birth to a son. Name him Jesus (which means, 'Yahweh saves'), because he will save the people from their sins.”

  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/23-03|[23-03]]]** So **Joseph**  married Mary and took her home as his wife, but he did not sleep with her until she had given birth.

  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/23-04|[23-04]]]****Joseph**  and Mary had to make a long journey from where they lived in Nazareth to Bethlehem because their ancestor was David whose hometown was Bethlehem.

  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/26-04|[26-04]]]** Jesus said, “The words I just read to you are happening right now.” All the people were amazed. “Isn't this the son of **Joseph**?” they said.
