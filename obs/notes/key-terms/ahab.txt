====== अहाब ======

===== कुछ तथ्य: =====

अहाब, उत्तर इस्राएल पर राज्य करनेवाला एक अत्यन्त ही दुष्ट राजा था |

  *

King Ahab was wicked and encouraged the people of the kingdom of Israel to worship false gods.

  *

The prophet Elijah confronted Ahab and told him that there would be a severe drought for three and a half years as a punishment for the sins that Ahab caused Israel to commit.

  *

Ahab had 450 prophets to the false god named Baal. Elijah challenged those prophets and proved that Yahweh is the only true God.

===== Examples from the Bible stories: =====

  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/19-02|[19-02]]]** Elijah was a prophet when **Ahab**  was king over the kingdom of Israel. **Ahab**  was an evil man who encouraged people to worship a false god named Baal.

  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/19-03|[19-03]]]****Ahab**  and his army looked for Elijah, but they could not find him.

  *

**[[https://door43.org/en/obs/notes/frames/19-05|[19-05]]]** After three and a half years, God told Elijah to return to the kingdom of Israel and speak with **Ahab**  because he was going to send rain again.
