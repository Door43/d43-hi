====== Jesus Was Changed [36-02] ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-36-02.jpg?nolink}}

As **[[:en:key-terms:jesus|Jesus]]** was praying, his face became as bright as the sun and his clothes became as white as light, **whiter** than anyone on earth could make them.


===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **whiter ** - That is, "more white."
