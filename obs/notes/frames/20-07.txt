====== बेबीलोन ने यहूदा का नाश किया [20-07] ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-20-07.jpg?nolink&}}

लेकिन कुछ साल बाद, **यहूदा** के राजा ने **बेबीलोन** के विरुद्ध **द्रोह** कर दिया। So, the Babylonians **came back** and attacked the kingdom of Judah. They **captured the city** of **[[:en:key-terms:jerusalem|Jerusalem]]**, destroyed the **[[:en:key-terms:temple|Temple]]**, and **took away** all the treasures of the city and the Temple.

===== Translation Notes: =====

  * **came back**  - This may also be translated as, "returned" or "came to Judah again."
  * **captured the city**  - That is, “took control of the city and the people there.”
  * **took away**  - They carried the treasures with them back to Babylon.
