===== Upload Your Work =====

This module answers the question: //How do I upload my work?//

Before you start this module have you learned about:
  * //[[hi:ta:vol1:tech:ts_share|Backup and Share Your Work]]//

==== Uploading ====

When you work in translationStudio, your text is saved every five minutes to your device. Your work is not saved to Door43 until you upload it. See [[hi:ta:vol1:tech:ts_publish]] for more information.
